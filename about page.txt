              ENDLESS GALAXY WARS 

Endless Galaxy Wars is a retro style space shooter where you are traveling through space to destroy all aliens from the known universe 

KEY ASPECTS OF THE GAME 

.Eliminate alien spaceships

.Dodge or destroying asteroids

.3 lives so be careful

.Take in turns with your friends and see who can get the best score
